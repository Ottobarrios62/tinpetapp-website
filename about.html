Our mission

Connecting pets, one match at a time

TinPet was created because we believe pets are happiest when they have friends. We saw how hard it was for pet owners to find compatible playmates nearby — and decided to fix that.

From a simple swipe mechanic inspired by the best of social apps, we built a platform that puts your pet's social life first. Match with nearby pets, chat with owners, schedule meetups, and discover the best local pet services — all in one place.

We're just getting started. Our vision is to be the go-to app for every pet owner who wants to give their furry friend the happiest, most connected life possible.
